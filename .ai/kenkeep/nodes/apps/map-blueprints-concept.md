Blueprints are reusable app templates defining required/public services, port mappings, lifecycle actions (PostCreate, PostRun, PostRebuild), and custom actions per service. Available via `blueprint:list`/`blueprint:info` and `GET /api/v1/authenticated/blueprints`.
